How did we work it out?

Thousands: 3 (odd, prime, multiples are 6, 9, 12)

Hundreds: 6 (even, divisible by 3 and 6, 600 ÷ 3 = 200)

Tens: 2 (even, smallest even number, 100 ÷ 2 = 50)

Ones: 5 (factor of numbers ending in 0, odd, prime, not factor of 12)
